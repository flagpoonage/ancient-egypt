Horus Den

Previous
Next
List

Horus Den, the fifth king of the 1st Dynasty, came to power when he was still a child, with his mother, Merneith, acting as queen-regent in his place. He was the first king to have used the title “King of Upper and Lower Egypt” and to have been represented wearing the Double Crown.
