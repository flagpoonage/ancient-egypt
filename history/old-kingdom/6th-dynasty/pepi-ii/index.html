Pepi II Quick Facts

Chronology

  • Dynasty: 6th
  • Predecessor: Merenre I
  • Successor: Merenre II
  • Highest Year: year after 31st census


Family

  • Father: Pepi I or Merenre I
  • Mother: Ankhenespepi I
  • Wives: Neith, Iput II, Ankhenespepi III, Ankhenespepi IV, Udjebten
  • Child: Merenre II (?), Neferkare III
