Khefren Quick Facts

Chronology

  • Dynasty: 4th
  • Predecessor: Djedefre
  • Successor: Bakare (?)
  • Highest Year: 13th Cattle Count
  • Reign: 2518 - 2492 BC.


Family

  • Father: Kheops
  • Mother: Henutsen
  • Wives: Khamernebti I, Meresankh III
  • Children: Duaenre, Khamernebti II, Khenterka, Mykerinos, Nebemakhet, Nikawre, Niuserre, Sekhemkare and Shepsestkau
