  • Dynasty: 4th
  • Predecessor: Snofru
  • Successor: Djedefre
  • Highest Year: 13th Cattle Count
  • Reign: 2551 - 2528 BC.


Family

  • Father: Snofru
  • Mother: Hetepheres I
  • Wives: Henutsen (?), Meretites
  • Children: Djedefre, Hetepheres II, Horbaf, Hordjedef, Kawab, Khamernebti I, Khefren, Khufu-khaf I, Meresankh II
