Cleopatra: Destiny's Queen - 1994

Biography offers a fascinating portrait of the last Queen of the Nile, from her noble birth to her legendary suicide. Ancient records combined with modern reenactments and computer-generated graphics bring her remarkable story to life.


Cleopatra - First Woman of Power - USA, 1999

Cast: Angelica Houston (narrator)

Cleopatra: The First Woman of Power examines this fascinating queen's life through contemporary Egyptian sources, from her Greek birth through her canny 20-year rule of Egypt.


Cleopatra's Alexandria - USA, 2000

The story of Alexandria, the city founded by Alexander the Great and capital of queen Cleopatra.


Intimate Portrait: Cleopatra - USA, 1998

Cast: Shari Belafonte(narrator)

Shari Belafonte tells the tale of Egypt's last queen. This documentary tries to separate the myth from the truth about this captivating woman, her marriages with her brothers, her relationships with Julius Caesar and Marc Antony, but also her abilities as a ruler of one of the world's greatest countries.
