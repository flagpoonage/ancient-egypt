Almost 60 Royal Mummies Found In Valley Of The Kings

Previous
Next
List

Egypt’s Minister of Antiquities has announced the find of a cache containing several wooden sarcophagi and cartonnage mummy masks. The cache also contains about 60 mummies, many holding titles as ‘prince’ or ‘princess’, members of the households of Thutmosis IV and his son Amenhotep III of the 18th Dynasty. This find will have an impact on our knowledge of the royal family of this era.
