List


Ancient Egypt at the Louvre

Authors: Andreu, Guillemette - Ziegler, Christiane e.a.
Publisher: Paris, 1997

Catalogue of one of the world's leading Ancient Egyptian collections outside Egypt. Also published in French.


Catalogue of Egyptian Art - Cleveland Museum

Authors: Berman, Lawrence Michael e.a.
Publisher: Hudson Hills, 1999

This magnificent catalogue describes over 500 objects of art on display at the Cleveland Museum, a collection that is considered one of the finest in the world.


Egyptian Art at Eaton College Selections from the Myers Museum

Authors: Spurr, Stephen e.a.
Publisher: St. Martins, 1999

The Myers Museum at Eaton College hosts one of the world's finest collections of Egyptian art.


Egyptian Art in the Age of the Pyramids

Authors: Arnold, Dorothea - Ziegler, Christiane e.a.
Publisher: Harry N. Abrams, 1999

Catalogue for the exhibition "Egyptian Art in the Age of the Pyramids", this book provides a great overview of the artistic achievements of the 3rd Dynasty and the Old Kingdom.


Egyptian Painting and Drawing in the British Museum

Authors: James, T.G.H.
Publisher: Hudson Hills, 1999

An overview of the Ancient Egyptian paintings and drawings at the British Museum. Part of a series of catalogues about the Egyptian collection of that museum.


Egyptian Treasures from the Egyptian Museum in Cairo

Author: Tiradritti, Francesco (ed)
Publisher: Harry N. Abrams, 1999

This magnificent catalogue goes through the highlights the Egyptian Museum in Cairo. Well-written and with many high-quality pictures, this book is a must for everybody with a keen interest in Egypt and its culture.


Eternal Egypt - Masterworks of Ancient Art from the British Museum

Author: Russmann, Edna R.
Publisher: University of California Press, 2001

An introduction into the vast legacy of Ancient Egypt, this book illustrates the development and achievements of ancient Egyptian art.


Gifts of the Nile: Faience from Ancient Egypt

Author: Friedman, Florence
Publisher: Thames & Hudson, 1998

The catalog with the acclaimed exhibition about Ancient Egyptian masterpieces of art created in faience.


Mistress of the House, Mistress of Heaven: Women in Ancient Egypt

Author: Capel, Anne K. (ed.)
Publisher: Hudson Hills, 1997

Catalogue for an exhibition examining and highlighting the role of women in the Ancient Egyptian society and religion.



Splendors of Ancient Egypt

Author: Peck, William H. e.a.
Publisher: Detroit Institute of Arts, 1997

Guide to the exhibit of the treasures of the Romer-Pelizaeus Museum in Hildesheim in Germany, this book contains many pictures of rare and rarely published objects.


The Royal Women of Amarna: Images of Beauty from Ancient Egypt

Authors: Arnold, Dorothea
Publisher: Abrams, 1996

The royal women played an important part in the so-called Amarna-revolution and at Amarna. This catalogue highlights their role and shows several of the most beautiful objects of art from that period.
