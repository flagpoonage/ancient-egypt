List


Amenhotep III: Egypt's Radiant Pharaoh

Author: Kozloff, Arielle P.
Publisher: Cambridge University Press, 2012

This book follows the life story of Amenhotep III, one of the most important rulers of ancient Egypt, who ruled for 38 years during the apex of Egypt's international and artistic power. 



Amenhotep III: Perspectives on His Reign

Author: O'Connor, David B. (ed.)
Publisher: University of Michigan Press, 1998

Amenhotep III, the father of Akhenaton, ruled Egypt at the high-point of its power and influence, during the 18th Dynasty. This book provides an in-depth study of the reign of this illustrious king.


The Annals Of Thutmose III The Egyptian World Conqueror

Author: Horne, Charles F.
Publisher: Kessinger Publishing LLC, 2010

Horne examines the royal annals of Thutmosis III, one of the most interesting historical documents of Ancient Egypt.



Hatchepsut. The Female Pharaoh

Author: Tyldesley, Joyce
Publisher: Penguin Books, 1998

An absorbing scholarly biography about Hatshepsut, a remarkable woman who ruled as pharaoh for 20 years in Egypt's 18th Dynasty. Tyldesley gives several fascinating new interpretations of Hatshepsut's life.

Horemheb: The Forgotten Pharaoh

Author: Booth, Charlotte
Publisher: Amberley, 2010

Horemheb, a general of the Egyptian army during the reign of Tutankhamun, ascended the throne after the death of Tutankhamun's successor, Ay, and laid the foundations for the 19th Dynasty.



Poisoned Legacy: The Fall of the Nineteenth Egyptian Dynasty

Author: Dodson, Aidan
Publisher: The American University Press in Cairo, 2010

After the death of Rameses II, the 19th Dynasty soon fell into decline and familial conflict, culminating in a final civil war that ended with the accession of a new dynasty. 


Ramesses II: The Greatest of Pharaohs

Author: Menu, Bernadette
Publisher: Harry N. Abrams, 1999

An in-depth study of the life and times of Ramesses II, who ruled Egypt at the highpoint of its power during the New Kingdom.


Ramesses III: The Life and Times of Egypt's Last Hero

Authors: Cline, Eric H. e.a.
Publisher: University of Michigan Press, 2012

Ramesses III is often considered the last great pharaoh of Egypt. After his death, the Egyptian empire collapsed and a divided country fell prey to foreign invasions.


Tausret: Forgotten Queen and Pharaoh of Egypt

Author: Wilkinson, Richard H. (ed.)
Publisher: Oxford University Press, 2012

One of only a few women who ruled ancient Egypt as a king during its thousands of years of history, Tausret was the last pharaoh of the 19th dynasty, the last ruling descendent of Ramesses the Great, and one of only two female monarchs buried in the Valley of the Kings.


Thutmose III: A New Biography

Authors: Cline, Eric H. - O'Connor, David (ed.)
Publisher: University of Michigan Press, 2006

Thutmosis III was one of Egypt's greatest pharaohs. This extensive book includes an overview of his life, and detailed examinations of civil administration, the religion and cults, architecture, artistic production, the military campaigns, as well as the Aegean and other foreign visitors to Egypt during Thutmosis' time.


Thutmose III: The Military Biography of Egypt's Greatest Warrior King

Author: Gabriel, Richard A.
Publisher: Potomac Books Inc., 2009

In this book, Richard Gabriel deftly brings to life the character and ability of Ancient Egypt’s warrior king and sheds light on Thutmosis’ key contributions to Egyptian history.
